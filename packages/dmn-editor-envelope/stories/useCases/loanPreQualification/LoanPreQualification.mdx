## Loan Pre Qualification

Is a diagram from a finance domain. The loan is qualified according to multiple inputs:

- `Applicant Data`
- `Requested Product`
- `Credit Score`

The qualification result is returned as a record of two entries:

- `Qualification`: that may be `"Qualified"` or `"Not Qualified"`
- `Reason`: that is short textual explanation of the `Qualification`
